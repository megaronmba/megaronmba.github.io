How do I get their feedback? I imagine that it is difficult; after all, I am planning to get feedback from “neutral” people, people who do not know the process.
Ask them to read the 3 STORIES in its entirety. The strength is in the whole. Each Wise Man has to answer the following questions after reading all 3 STORIES:
    Question 1: Images have the power to perpetuate an idea in the reader’s mind. Once you read my 3 STORIES and turn off your computer, what IMAGE you could you build of me?

    Question 2: Through all 3 STORIES, I tried to reinforce that I am competent in 2-3 Strengths/Abilities/Features. Mention 2-3 Competences that come to your mind after the reading.

    Question 3: I tried to bring COHERENCE among the different stories. Is there one single ESSENCE from all 3 STORIES?

    Question 4: You know me through different dimensions. Which element is missing? How do you think it could be contextualized? What is your suggestion for the 4 STORIES to transmit one sole MESSAGE?

    Question 5: You probably know that rational arguments only do not convince people. Do you see any “emotional resonance” in my 3 STORIES? Do I look like an honest person in my writing?
Do you have any further tips to offer?
Remember: the focus of the exercise must not be particular criticism. Ask them to focus not on the ANALYSIS but rather on the SYNTHESIS of your text.
