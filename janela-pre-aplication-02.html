Every well-prepared applicant should know how to answer 16 MBA Application Themes:
 
Theme 1.) Give me ONE example of when you solved an analytical problem.
Theme 2.) Give me ONE example of when you showed creativity or did something out of curiosity.
Theme 3.) Give me ONE example of when you efficiently executed a plan.
Theme 4.) Give me ONE example of when you played a leader’s role.
Theme 5.) Give me ONE example of when you had to make a decision without having all the information you needed.
Theme 6.) Give me ONE example of when you questioned an established practice or a thought within a group/organization.
Theme 7.) Give me ONE example of when you persuaded someone or a group of people.
Theme 8.) Give me ONE example of when you showed concern about people around you.
Theme 9.) Give me ONE example of when you faced a setback or a failure.
Theme 10.) Describe an extra-professional activity in which you have been or are still involved for a significant time.
Theme 11.) What are you most proud of?
Theme 12.) Walk me through your resume.
Theme 13.) Tell me about a situation when you realized you needed an MBA to attain your objectives in life?
Theme 14.) Imagine yourself as a member of our B-school X community; tell me three activities you would get involved with.
Theme 15.) What do you want to do upon MBA graduating?
Theme 16.) What is the weakness of your application?
